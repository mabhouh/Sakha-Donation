The Red Crescent Society of the United Arab Emirates is the United Arab Emirates (UAE) affiliate of the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies.

The authority was founded in Abu Dhabiin 1983 with support of the late Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan. Its main objectives are to carry out Red Cross operations on a systematic basis throughout the UAE.

The importance of volunteering for humanitarian work:-
There is no doubt that volunteer work is of great importance and positively influential in the life of the individual and society, as no human society is without volunteer work, which is a fundamental pillar of development and progress, and one of the advantages of volunteer work is: - - Improving the economic and social level and what follows in improving living conditions. - Preserving authentic values in society and stimulating entrepreneurship. - Reviving and embodying the principle of social solidarity. - Investing leisure time. Volunteering is an ancient human practice, when good people are done and why it gives a feeling of happiness when doing it.Humanitarian work is always in need of volunteers and white hands because humanitarian work is many and complex. Number of volunteers affiliated with the Authority:- Nearly 3,000 volunteers are on the rise. Examples of volunteer participation in humanitarian work:- Participation in local seasonal campaigns such as: - Distribution of sacrificial meat on Eid al-Adha - Distribution of Ramadan Mir - Iftar program of the fasting person, fundraising campaigns and in-kind assistance. - Participation in committees of decision, humanitarian and medical aid (committees in branches). - Participation in the implementation of social research for humanitarian groups (social research in branches) - Participation in external relief programs and presence in places of disasters and calamities to provide a helping hand participation in the Syrian refugee camp in Jordan - Participation in the clothing of one million children around the world. How to catch up with humanitarian work:- To join humanitarian work, an individual must join the Emirates Red Crescent Authority as a volunteer, and of course, there are conditions that must be adhered to to be accepted as a volunteer in the Authority, which is: - He must be a citizen of the country, the Cooperation Council or a resident who have a valid residence. - He must be able to participate in volunteer programs. - Good conduct and behavior. - Not less than (18) years old and not more than (50) (on recommendation). - Adhering to the principles of the International Federation. - The educational qualification is not less than high school. - Obtaining a license to practice the profession from the competent authority in the country if practicing that profession requires this and his volunteering is limited to the field of His profession After acceptance, he will be contacted and the volunteer will be used to participate in the humanitarian programs implemented by the authority. To join as a volunteer, you must visit one of the Authority's branches spread in all emirates of the country or access the UAE Red Crescent Authority's electronic page to fill out the application for affiliation and proceed with the required procedures.
